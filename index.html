Bring on the Chaos!

Welcome to the Chaos Music Festival of 2013 where alternative rock lovers can get their groove on! This year's festival will take place in beautiful Central Park this summer in NYC! The festival will be take place across four days in June starting on June 20th and ending on June 23rd. This year's line-up includes Maroon 5 with their awesome front-man Adam Levine, the soothing rhymes of OneRepublic, and the earth-shattering sound of Skillet. This year's festivities will include a Karaoke contest where fans can duet with their favorite musicians and also the bands will be giving out free music lessons where fans can learn how to play certain instruments like the guitar with ease. It's gonna be a good life like OneRepublic says. Rock On!

The Bands

Maroon 5

With the charisma of their front-man Adam Levine and their signature pop-guitar sound, Maroon 5 continues to be awesome with love as the primary theme in their music.





OneRepublic

With their urban style and their laidback approach, OneRepublic creates great music that soothes the soul reminding everyone to take it easy now and then.





Skillet

A Christian rock band that's knows no limit. With their chaotic beats and heavy sound, Skillet shatters the stage every time.
